Why is it important to meditate?

Meditation dates back to ancient cultures, and has been proven to have effective outcomes, such as heightened concentration. Meditation stems from the Latin term, 'meditatum', which means 'to ponder'. Several hieroglyphs artefacts illustrate that hunter gatherers were amongst the first to meditate.

Evidence of importance of meditation

Through meditation, studies have shown a significant decrease in stress and anxiety levels, as well as a heightened degree of self awareness, improved physical and emotional health, as well as mental health.
